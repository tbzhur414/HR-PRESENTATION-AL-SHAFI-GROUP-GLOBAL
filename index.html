Assalamualaikum and welcome to Alshafi Group's HR and Corporate Sales Outlook Plan. Today we proudly present our strategic initiatives for the new year to achieve excellence in Human Resources Management and Corporate Sales.

Comprehensive Human Resources Initiatives

1

Interdepartmental Meetings

Increasing from 5 to 12 meetings per department annually for better collaboration and performance.

2

Professional Hirings

Rigorous selection with tests on Current Affairs, Pakistan Affairs, Science, Accounting, and Fertilizers. Only candidates scoring above 50% proceed.

3

Record Management

All employee records will be maintained digitally through our secure HR Portal.

4

Performance Appraisal

Daily self-appraisals via portal, reviewed by managers and HR for continuous feedback.

5

Training Sessions

Monthly Professional Ethics training. Sales team receives specialized training twice monthly.

6

Departmental Successors

Managers will identify and prepare high-potential successors for leadership continuity.

Additional Strategic HR Initiatives

7

Digital Transformation & Portal Enhancements

Advanced analytics dashboards for real-time insights into employee performance and engagement.

8

Employee Wellness Programs

Regular wellness workshops, mental health support, and fitness initiatives.

9

Diversity & Inclusion Drive

Promoting diversity in hiring and an inclusive workplace culture.
